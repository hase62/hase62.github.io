ABOUT

I'm an assistant professor at Tohoku university and co-founder/analysis advisor at miup Inc. I'm interested in applying technological developments to society for the innovation of human life and my majors are statistical science, machine lerning, stochastic simulation, computational biology and bioinformatics. My interest also includes modern history, calssical strategy, e.g., Hundred Schools of Thought and The Prince, geopolitics and modern philosophy. I have also skills of developing several types of artificial intelligence, e.g., Bayesian inference and deep learning, and analyzing several types of Big Data (n is Very Large) and High Dimensional Data, e.g., whole genome/exome data, epigenome data and biological observation data.
