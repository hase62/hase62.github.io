Takanori Hasegawa, Ph.D

Assistant Professor at The Univerity of Tokyo, Technology Advisor at CRUNCHERS Inc.

ABOUT

I'm an assistant professor at Tohoku university and a technical advisor at CRUNCHERS Inc and Medley, Inc. I'm expert of statistical science, machine learning, stochastic simulation, computational biology and bioinformatics, and interested in applying technological developments in computer science and biotechnology to society for the innovation of human life. In academic research, I'm focusing on inferring and reconstructing intracellular systems by developing statistical methodologies to integrate biological data and knowledge and also analyzing human genome data to elucidate association between variants and phenotypes. In business field, my main works are to develop artificial intelligence using, e.g., Bayesian inference and deep learning, and to analyze several types of Big Data and High Dimensional Data. My interest also includes modern history, classical strategy, e.g., Hundred Schools of Thought and The Prince, geopolitics and philosophy.

Activity
